# Account Settings

Personal settings for your BluePLM account.

## Profile

Update your user profile:
- **Full Name** - How you appear to other users
- **Email** - Your sign-in email
- **Avatar** - Profile picture (from Google or uploaded)

## Preferences

Customize your experience:

### Language
Choose from supported languages:
- English
- Chinese (Simplified/Traditional)
- German
- French
- Spanish
- Portuguese

### Display
- **Theme** - Dark mode (light theme coming soon)
- **Sidebar mode** - Expanded, collapsed, or expand on hover

## Keybindings

Customize keyboard shortcuts for common actions:
- File operations (checkout, checkin, download)
- Navigation
- View toggles

Click a keybinding to record a new shortcut.

## Modules

Control which modules appear in the sidebar:
- Drag to reorder
- Toggle visibility
- Create custom groups
- Add dividers between sections

### Module Configuration
Each module can have:
- **Custom icon** - Choose from available icons
- **Custom color** - Change the icon color
- **Parent group** - Nest under another module

## Delete Account

Permanently delete your account and all associated data.

::: danger This cannot be undone
Deleting your account removes:
- Your user profile
- Your contributions
- Your settings

Files you've uploaded remain in the organization's vaults.
:::
